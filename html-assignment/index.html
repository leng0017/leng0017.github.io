About Me

First, let me introduce myself, my name is Yue, come from China. It is my 10th year in Canada, finished my high school in PEI, which is a beautiful little island to me and now I'm in Ottawa. Ottawa is such a big city compared to Charlottetown. The biggest thing I feel better in Ottawa is the efficiency of public service, such as snow removal. Once there was a snow storm in Charlottetown, and the snow removal truck came like Two Days later! Which could never happen in Ottawa. Anyway, glad I came to Ottawa.

My favourite thing in the world is traveling. Traveling means a lot to me, because in my opinion, life is short, and it is necessary to see what the world looks like within a limited time. Meeting different people, experencing different cultures and visiting different types of lands are my ultimate goals.

A hangover suggests a great night, jet lag suggests a great adventure. -J.D.Andrews
